What is Tether USD (BSC Peg)?

Tether USD (BSC Peg) is a synthetic, USD‑pegged stablecoin running on the Binance Smart Chain (BEP‑20). It is designed to maintain a soft 1:1 parity with the US Dollar while taking advantage of BSC's low fees and fast confirmations.

The token can be used for payments, trading, and DeFi strategies in the BSC ecosystem. Peg stability is supported through liquidity pools and market‑based mechanisms on decentralized exchanges.

Fast & Low‑Cost

Enjoy low gas fees and fast confirmations on BNB Smart Chain compared to legacy networks.

DeFi Ready

Integrates easily into AMMs, lending platforms, and other DeFi protocols in the BSC ecosystem.

Stable‑Value Design

Aim to track the value of 1 USD with reduced volatility via on‑chain liquidity and market incentives.
